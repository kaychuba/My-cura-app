# Data Processing Agreement (DPA) — Template

> **Status: DRAFT template for legal review.** Executed between My-Cura Ltd
> (**Processor**) and each care agency customer (**Controller**) alongside
> the service agreement. UK GDPR art. 28 terms.

**Parties:** [Agency legal name] (Controller) and My-Cura Ltd (Processor).

1. **Subject matter & duration.** Processing of the Controller's care
   management data on the My-Cura platform for the term of the service
   agreement.
2. **Nature & purpose.** Hosting, storage, display and processing of care
   records, workforce and payroll data, as instructed through the
   platform's features. Data categories and subjects: as per the ROPA
   (Annex A).
3. **Controller instructions.** The Processor processes personal data only
   on documented instructions (use of the platform constitutes such
   instructions) and informs the Controller if an instruction appears to
   infringe data-protection law.
4. **Confidentiality.** Persons authorised to process the data are bound by
   confidentiality obligations.
5. **Security (art. 32).** The Processor implements and maintains the
   measures in Annex B, including: per-tenant row-level isolation, mandatory
   MFA for administrative access, Argon2id password hashing, AES-256
   encryption of sensitive fields and backups, append-only audit and
   consent logs, rate limiting, vulnerability scanning and patch SLAs.
6. **Sub-processors.** Listed in Annex C; the Processor gives 30 days'
   notice of changes, during which the Controller may object on reasonable
   grounds. Sub-processors are bound by equivalent terms.
7. **Data-subject rights.** The Processor assists the Controller with
   access, rectification, erasure, restriction and portability requests via
   platform functionality or export within 10 business days.
8. **Personal-data breach.** The Processor notifies the Controller without
   undue delay and within 48 hours of becoming aware, with the information
   required by art. 33(3), and assists with regulator notifications.
9. **DPIA assistance.** The Processor provides reasonable assistance with
   DPIAs and prior consultations.
10. **Return & deletion.** On termination, the Controller may export all
    tenant data; the Processor deletes it within 90 days except where law
    requires retention, and certifies deletion on request.
11. **Audit.** The Processor makes available compliance information
    (security documentation, pen-test summaries, certifications) and allows
    audits with 30 days' notice, at most annually unless following a breach.
12. **International transfers.** Data is hosted in the UK/EEA. No transfer
    outside without the Controller's authorisation and appropriate
    safeguards.
13. **Liability.** As per the service agreement. *(Legal review.)*

**Annex A** — ROPA extract. **Annex B** — docs/SECURITY.md +
docs/DATA-PROTECTION.md. **Annex C** — sub-processor list.
